Chikos Gourmet wanted to move away from PDF menus and a static site. They needed an experience that reflected the care in every dish, plus an internal tool to update prices and photos in minutes.

## End-to-end editable system

I built an admin panel where the team can update dishes, photos, locations, and campaigns. Images are stored and served via Cloudflare Images with device-optimized variants.

## Key decisions

- Remix architecture for SSR without hurting SEO.
- Image optimization with AVIF/WebP variants.
- Admin panel with per-branch management.
- Versioned menu system for seasonal campaigns.
