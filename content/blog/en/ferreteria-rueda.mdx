Ferretería Rueda had operated in Barranquilla for over 30 years with no digital presence beyond social media. The goal: build a complete, functional online sales channel aligned with their physical catalog.

## Shopify + Liquid from scratch

My first experience with Liquid. I learned its structures (`sections`, `snippets`), conditionals, and reusable block system. I combined this with custom JavaScript to improve UX without breaking Shopify's native compatibility.

## What I built

- Dynamic product carousel on the homepage.
- Hierarchical dropdown menus by category.
- Custom filters on product listing pages.
- Functional search by name or keyword.
- Automatic discount badges on sale products.
- Flexible interface: admins rearrange sections without code.

<Callout>
  The result is a digital catalog that reflects the breadth of the physical inventory, with a clear shopping experience for customers who previously only knew the brick-and-mortar store.
</Callout>
